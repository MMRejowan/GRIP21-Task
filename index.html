About Us

We are a small organisation and collaborate with a care center for stray animals.
Our objective is to find foster homes for these needy animals and also nurture those who are staying in the care home.
The organisation was founded by couple of students and is managed virtually. The care centers are privately owned by people around the globe.
We act as a medium between the care centers and people by collecting the donation and sending it to the care centers.
